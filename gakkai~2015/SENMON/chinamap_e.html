China Map Information
　　Chief : Kenichi SHIBATA　　Vice Chief : Tadamitsu SAITO

Activity Plan    

The purpose, the contents, the necessity, etc. for activity  The relation between Japan and China has greeted important aspects of affairs, such as economic industry and natural environment. China map and map data came to be gradually exhibited for internationalization in China. However, detailed map information may has a severe rule in and outside the country in order that a complicated political situation. In order that a sectional meeting group may study the present condition of China map information regulation, the limit of map information in which the present acquisition is possible, a rule, etc. and may aim at much more spread use of the China map information from now on, it adjusts a research result and performs an announcement of the society.
The activity schedule for two years

2007： The warm-up meeting of study preparation about the present condition of the China map information acquisition and the practical-use example of the China map is scheduled once.

2008： The study meeting about the directivity of the future of freedom of information for China map, and about the spread and the utilizing method of China maps is scheduled 4 times per year.
